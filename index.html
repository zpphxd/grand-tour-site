Ready-made clusters

    Money-saving moves

      Seasonal price rule: put Southern Europe (Spain, Italy, the south of France) in the cheaper shoulder season, October to April, and save summer for the north — the UK, Scandinavia, the Alps. Pre-book event-locked trips such as Oktoberfest, San Fermín, Cannes and Glastonbury three to four months out, or sleep one town over.
